Privacy Policy for WriteBoost

Effective Date: September 13, 2025

1. Introduction

WriteBoost ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when you use the WriteBoost Chrome Extension. By installing and using WriteBoost, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

WriteBoost is designed to enhance your writing experience. We strive to minimize the collection of personal data. Specifically:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or contact details) unless you voluntarily provide it to us through support requests.
  • Usage Data: Some non-personal information, such as error logs or performance metrics, may be collected to improve stability and performance. This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you.
  • Local Processing: Text that you enter or highlight in the extension is processed locally in your browser. Unless explicitly stated, this text is not transmitted to external servers.

3. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the functionality of the WriteBoost extension.
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide technical support.
  • To analyze anonymous usage trends to make improvements.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To enforce our terms of service or protect our legal rights.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case users will be notified.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal or sensitive data on our servers. Any text processed by WriteBoost remains in your local browser environment unless you explicitly choose to share it with us for support purposes.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

WriteBoost is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of WriteBoost after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
